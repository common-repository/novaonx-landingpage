== Installation ==

1. Go to Plugins in your WordPress administration, click Add New, and search for “NovaonX Landing Page WordPress Plugin”.
2. Now click at Install Now button.
3. Click on Activate after upload has completed.
4. Go to Settings and choose NovaonX Landingpage.
5. Log into your Landing Page account at https://landingpage.novaonx.com/
6. When publishing your landing page from NovaonX Landing Page, change thirdparty tab, choose WordPress.
